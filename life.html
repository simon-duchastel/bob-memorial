Bob's Life

Early Years

Bob came into our lives as a tiny ball of fur with oversized paws and a curious spirit. From the start, he was fearless—exploring every corner of the house, climbing curtains, and claiming the best spot on the couch.

Personality

Bob was calm, loyal, and surprisingly polite. He never met a lap he didn't like, and he had a talent for showing up exactly when you needed him most. He communicated with slow blinks, head bumps, and the occasional 3 a.m. zoomies.

Later Years

As he grew older, Bob slowed down but never lost his charm. He traded high shelves for warm beds, and his playful swats became gentle paws on your arm. He aged with grace, teaching us how to be present and patient.
