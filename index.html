In Memory of Bob

Obituary

Bob passed away peacefully, surrounded by love. He was more than a pet—he was family, a constant presence through life's joys and sorrows. With his gentle purr and endless patience, he brought comfort to everyone who knew him.

He loved sunny windowsills, cardboard boxes, and the sound of a can opening at 6 a.m. He lived a full life of naps, adventures, and quiet companionship. He is deeply missed and forever remembered.
